We are a nationwide ministry in Uganda in which thousands of children will reap the benefits of one manâ€™s clear God-given vision. Three years ago, Mr. Mugizi Jackson, a former district planner Bushenyi district in Uganda became increasingly troubled by the sight of thousands of poor, orphaned and other vulnerable children in Uganda living on streets and abandoned by society. They had no food, no security, no education, no clothes and no care at all from any source. This situation is due to absolute poverty, domestic violence, and diseases (especially HIV/AIDs which cause several deaths of parents in homes leaving behind many orphans with no one to care for them). Municipal workers of one town one day, collected several dead bodies of some of these children and put them on the truck. These had died as a result of thunderstorms that hit them in the previous day. Looking at them, Mr. Mugizi Jackson thought of how to bring life again for these children. He decided to leave his lucrative job to form CRELAM (Create Life Again Ministries), an organization that would see the situation of these children change for better. He used his gratuity proceeds to start this organization. Today, 170 children are receiving full education in a primary school and it is hoped that the future of these children will be better.
We are a Christian non-profit making organization and a child advocacy ministry pairing people who have compassion with the poor, orphaned and other vulnerable children so as to release them from spiritual, economic, social and physical poverty.
